   Large-Scale Mining of Statistically Significant Connected Subgraphs.

This system enables a user to mine large real-scale graphs online. The system also lets user understand the effects of graph parameters on the mining results by synthetically generating graphs of varying parameters. The graphs can be generated according to the basic random models - Erdős-Rényi and Barabási-Albert.
